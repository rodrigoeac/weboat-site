Questions about Trip Areas

WeBoat Brasil offers boat trips in several areas of Rio de Janeiro: Mureta da Urca, Praia Vermelha, Copacabana (including Arpoador and Ipanema), Ilhas Cagarras, Itaipu and Camboinhas (Niteroi), and all of Guanabara Bay. All trips depart from Marina da Gloria and can be customized to your preference.

For a first time, we recommend Mureta da Urca or Praia Vermelha. These are areas with calm waters inside Guanabara Bay, ideal for families and beginners. Check our available routes to choose the best one for you.

Yes! Our routes can be customized. The Full Tour, for example, combines Urca, Copacabana and Ilhas Cagarras in a single 5-hour trip. Talk to our team via WhatsApp to create the ideal route.

For diving and snorkeling, Ilhas Cagarras is the best option. The archipelago has crystal clear waters and abundant marine life. Itaipu and Camboinhas also offer excellent visibility for water activities.

For watching the sunset, we recommend the Copacabana route or Mureta da Urca. The view of the sun setting behind the mountains is spectacular. Schedule trips in the late afternoon to enjoy this unique experience.
