What is the Best Boat For...

What is the best boat for small groups (up to 15 people)?

The WeBoat 32 is the best option. Priced from R$ 2,300, it offers the best value in the fleet. Perfect for family trips or with friends.

View WeBoat 32

What is the best boat for parties and bachelorette parties?

The WeBoat 390 is the most recommended. With capacity for 16 people and versatile space, it is perfect for celebrations. All have a BBQ grill.

View WeBoat 390

What is the best boat for large groups (more than 20 people)?

The WeBoat Rio Star 50 is the only own option. Capacity for 22 people, from R$ 4,000. For larger groups, we have partner boats.

View Rio Star 50

What is the best boat for a premium trip?

The WeBoat Oceanic 36 offers the most sophisticated experience. Superior finish and greater comfort for up to 14 people, from R$ 2,400.

Frequently Asked Questions about Comparison

Prices from Monday to Thursday are promotional because demand is lower. Friday, Saturday and Sunday have high demand, which justifies different prices. By booking during the week, you can save up to R$ 500 on a single trip.

All 5 own boats include: fuel for the booked route, captain licensed by the Brazilian Navy, drink coolers (ice not included), Bluetooth sound system, floating mat, pool noodles, life jackets and certified safety equipment.

Yes, all 5 own boats have a BBQ grill. Use requires an additional service, with a fee ranging from R$ 250 to R$ 600 depending on the boat. The service includes meats, sides and preparation. Contact our team for more details.

We have partner boats for groups of up to 65 people. The Catamarã Oceano fits up to 65 people and the Barco Gourmet 53 up to 40 people. All vessels undergo the same safety inspection and offer the WeBoat quality standard. See all boats.
