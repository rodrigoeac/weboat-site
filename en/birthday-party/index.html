Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I have a BBQ on the boat?
Yes! We have boats with BBQ grills available, such as the WeBoat 390. The full BBQ service with grill master and meats starts from R$ 100 per person. You can also book just the BBQ grill fee and bring your own items.
How does birthday decoration work?
We offer the Premium Party Kit starting at R$ 1,850, including balloons, custom banner, decorated table, lighting and candles. You can also bring your own decoration at no extra cost.
Can I bring meu bolo de birthday?
Of course! You can bring your own cake. We have space to store it during the trip and the crew can help when it's time to sing happy birthday in a special moment.
What is the ideal time for a birthday on a boat?
The late afternoon time (from 3 PM onwards) is very popular for watching the sunset. We also do morning and afternoon trips. Weekends and holidays tend to be in higher demand, so book in advance.
Can I extend the trip duration?
Yes! You can book additional hours. Many birthday celebrants opt for 4h, 5h or up to 6h trips to enjoy more with BBQ and drinks. Check the prices for extra hours.
