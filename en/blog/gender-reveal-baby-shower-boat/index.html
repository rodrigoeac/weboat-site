Why Celebrate on a Boat?

Traditional venues like living rooms and restaurants are lovely, but a boat offers a setting that simply can't be replicated. The exclusivity, the surrounding nature and the 360-degree views of Rio's most iconic landmarks turn a party into a lifelong memory.

  • Unique setting: the sea, the mountains and the city as backdrop
  • Total privacy: only the people you invite are on board
  • Memorable experience: a party no one will ever forget
  • All-in-one venue: no need to rent a separate space for decor or catering
  • Ideal for small groups: up to 16 people on the WeBoat 390

Decoration on Board

Boat decor is completely doable and, precisely because of the space constraints, ends up more intimate and cozy. Here's what works perfectly at sea:

Classic "Team Blue or Team Pink"

  • Balloons: latex or foil balloons in neutral colors (gold, white, mint) or "Team Blue vs Team Pink" â€” tied to the stern rails
  • Fabric bunting: triangular flags in the baby's color palette strung along the boat
  • Table decoration: centerpiece with flowers, votives and the "big reveal box"
  • Photo corner: portable frame or fabric backdrop for photos with guests

What to Avoid at Sea

  • Confetti cannons that scatter into the water (environmental impact)
  • Loose paper confetti without a collection bag
  • Heavy or unstable decorations
  • Floating balloons released without a string (sea littering)

Pro Tip

Bring a confetti balloon â€” a transparent balloon filled with colored confetti with a smaller balloon inside. When burst, the confetti rains down inside the boat without polluting the sea. The reveal is spectacular!

Smoke Bombs: What Does the Law Say?

This is the most critical point and the most frequent question: are smoke bombs allowed on boats?

Important: Smoke Bombs Are Prohibited

Under Capitania dos Portos (Brazil's port authority) regulations, colored smoke devices are classified as distress signals on waterways. Using a smoke bomb on a boat can trigger emergency response from the Coast Guard and result in fines for the operator.

Safe alternatives: confetti balloons, a balloon box, colored powder in a sealed package opened at anchor in an authorized area, or a decorated cake with the reveal inside.

Safety for Expecting Mothers

The wellbeing of the mom-to-be is the absolute priority. WeBoat Brasil has hosted many pregnancy celebrations and our crew is trained to ensure maximum comfort and safety.

Before Booking

  • Consult your OB-GYN â€” most doctors clear boat trips up to around 32â€“34 weeks with no complications
  • Check the weather forecast: calm seas are essential (avoid swell above 0.5m)
  • If there is any nausea sensitivity, consider ginger tea or ask your doctor about anti-nausea medication

On the Day of the Event

  • Seating position: expecting mothers should sit in the center of the boat, at the widest point â€” the most stable spot
  • Avoid the bow: the front moves more in waves
  • Frequent breaks: the boat stops at designated points for leisure
  • Shade: request a parasol; sun exposure at sea is more intense
  • Hydration: always keep water at hand; the boat carries coolers
