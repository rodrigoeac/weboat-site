Music on Board: Playlists & Sound Tips for Your Boat Trip

Picture this: the sun warming your skin, a gentle ocean breeze, Guanabara Bay unfolding before your eyes -- and the perfect playlist setting the mood. Music and boat trips are an unbeatable combination, and at WeBoat we take that seriously. All of our boats come equipped with a built-in Bluetooth sound system, ready for you to connect and enjoy.

In this guide, we've put together practical tips on how to use the sound system on board, playlist suggestions for every type of occasion, and details about our professional DJ service. Everything so that the soundtrack of your trip is as unforgettable as the scenery.

Sound System: How It Works on WeBoat Boats

All boats in the WeBoat fleet come with a Bluetooth sound system already included in the trip price -- no extra cost. It's simple to use:

  • Standard Bluetooth: any phone (Android or iPhone) pairs with the boat's sound system in seconds
  • Connect before you set sail: our top tip is to pair your phone while still at the marina, with the crew member guiding you. That way, the music is already playing when the boat departs
  • Range: Bluetooth works perfectly across the entire boat -- at the bow, the stern, and the flybridge
  • Quality: the speakers are positioned to distribute audio evenly, even in windy conditions

Important tip: offline mode

Download your playlists in offline mode before boarding. Cell signal can be unreliable in open water, especially on the Ilhas Cagarras and Itaipu & Camboinhas routes. On Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube Music, just tap the download button on your playlist.

Playlists by Occasion: The Ideal Soundtrack

Every trip has its own vibe, and music makes all the difference in setting the right mood. Here are genre and style suggestions for the most popular occasions:

Bachelorette Party

A bachelorette party on a boat calls for high energy and lots of excitement. The playlist needs to get everyone up and dancing -- even in the middle of the ocean. Build your list with our complete bachelorette guide in hand, and don't forget:

  • Brazilian funk and pop: Anitta, Ludmilla, Pedro Sampaio, MC Cabelinho
  • International pop: Beyoncé, Dua Lipa, Shakira, Bad Bunny
  • Dance hits: those songs everyone knows the words to and sings along

Birthday Party

A birthday on a boat calls for an eclectic mix that appeals to all guests. Start mellow and gradually raise the energy throughout the trip. See more ideas in our birthday party guide:

  • Classic MPB (Brazilian Popular Music): Jorge Ben Jor, Tim Maia, Seu Jorge
  • Brazilian pop: Natiruts, Jota Quest, Paralamas
  • Funk and pagode: for the second half of the trip, when the energy is already high

Romantic Trip for Two

For a romantic couples' trip, the music should be soft and inviting. No loud volume here -- the sound plays a supporting role, not the lead:

  • Bossa nova: Tom Jobim, João Gilberto, Nara Leão
  • Romantic MPB: Djavan, Marisa Monte, Ana Carolina
  • Smooth jazz: Norah Jones, Diana Krall, Michael Bublé

Corporate Event

For a corporate event on a boat, background music should create a sophisticated atmosphere without competing with networking conversations:

  • Lounge and chill: Cafe del Mar, Buddha Bar, Hotel Costes
  • Light deep house: smooth beats, minimal vocals
  • Instrumental: piano jazz, classical guitar, instrumental bossa nova

Family with Kids

With children on board, the playlist needs to be upbeat and cheerful, but appropriate for all ages. Check out our guide on boat trips with kids:

  • Brazilian pop: Ivete Sangalo, Claudia Leitte, Melim
  • Light axé: Chiclete com Banana, Olodum, É O Tchan
  • Movie soundtracks: Disney, Pixar -- kids love singing along

Sunset

A sunset on the waters of Rio is a show in itself, and the right music transforms the moment into something truly cinematic:

  • Chill out and lo-fi: soft, atmospheric beats
  • Modern bossa nova: Ceu, Tiago Iorc, Silva
  • Soft electronic: Bonobo, Tycho, Khruangbin

Where to Build Your Playlists

We recommend putting your playlist together ahead of time on one of the streaming platforms. All of them work perfectly with the boats' Bluetooth:

  • Spotify: search for ready-made playlists like "Boat Party", "Brazilian Summer", "Bossa Nova Chill", or "Funk Hits"
  • Apple Music: themed sections like "Summer", "Party", and "Relax"
  • YouTube Music: a good option if you don't have a premium subscription -- but remember to download for offline use
Tip from the crew: build a playlist with at least 6 hours of music. The trip lasts 5 hours, and nobody wants the music to stop because the playlist ran out halfway through the afternoon.

Professional DJ Service on Board

For those who want to take the musical experience to the next level, WeBoat offers a professional DJ service as one of our add-on services:

  • Price: starting at R$ 800 (DJ + equipment)
  • Equipment: the DJ brings an extra speaker, controller, and full setup
  • Custom repertoire: the DJ talks to you before the trip to build the ideal set
  • Ideal for: bachelorette parties, birthday celebrations with lots of guests, and premium corporate events

The DJ takes care of everything: smooth transitions, reading the crowd's energy, and even a microphone for toasts and the birthday song. It's well worth it for groups of 15 or more who want to turn their trip into a true party at sea.

Volume and Courtesy: What You Need to Know

Enjoying loud music on a boat is part of the fun, but there are some common courtesy rules and regulations worth knowing:

  • Inside the marina: when departing from and returning to Marina da Glória, we ask that you keep the volume moderate out of respect for other vessels and establishments
  • While navigating: in open water, the music typically doesn't bother anyone -- feel free to turn it up
  • Near other vessels: when anchored near other boats (at Mureta da Urca, for example), use good judgment
  • Regulations: Brazil's Navy regulations (NORMAM) include rules about noise pollution in port areas. The crew member knows the rules and will guide you

In practice, there are rarely any issues with volume during the trip. The sound of the engine and the wind already create a natural buffer. The important thing is to enjoy yourself responsibly.

Frequently Asked Questions About Music on a Boat

Do WeBoat boats have a sound system?

Yes! All WeBoat boats come with a built-in Bluetooth sound system, included in the trip price. Just pair your phone and you're good to go. Learn more about how the trip works from start to finish.

Can I hire a DJ for the boat trip?

Yes. The professional DJ service on board starts at R$ 800 and includes their own equipment with an extra speaker. The DJ customizes the repertoire to match your occasion -- ideal for bachelorette parties and large birthday celebrations.

Do I need to download music before the trip?

We highly recommend it! Cell signal can be unreliable in open water. Download your playlists in offline mode on Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube Music before boarding.

Is there a volume limit for music on the boat?

In open water, there are no practical restrictions. Inside the marina and near other vessels, we ask for common courtesy. The crew member will advise on areas where it's best to keep the volume down.
