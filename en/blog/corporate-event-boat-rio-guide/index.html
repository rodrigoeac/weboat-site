How to Plan a Corporate Event on a Boat: Complete Guide

Organizing a corporate event on a boat in Rio de Janeiro is an increasingly popular choice among companies looking to break away from the conventional. Instead of hotel meeting rooms or restaurants, your team can celebrate achievements, strengthen relationships, and create unforgettable memories while cruising Guanabara Bay, with Sugarloaf Mountain and Christ the Redeemer as the backdrop.

This guide was designed for HR professionals, managers, and event planners who need to organize a flawless corporate event. Here you'll find everything: event types, boat capacity, catering options, equipment, invoicing, planning timeline, and team building activities.

Types of Corporate Events on a Boat

A private boat is versatile enough for different types of corporate events. The most common are:

  • Year-end celebration: the most popular format. Perfect for celebrating results, rewarding teams, and closing the year in style. Pairs perfectly with BBQ on board and open bar.
  • Integration and onboarding: ideal for welcoming new employees or bringing together teams from different departments. The relaxed atmosphere fosters genuine connections.
  • Team building: water activities, team challenges, and group dynamics that strengthen collaboration outside the office.
  • Awards and recognition: celebrate targets met, company anniversaries, or individual achievements in an exclusive setting.
  • Product launch: present brand novelties in a unique setting with high visual and experiential impact.
  • Floating happy hour: a more casual format, perfect for Fridays or quick celebrations with drinks and appetizers.
  • Executive meeting: board meetings or brainstorming sessions in an inspiring environment, away from office distractions.

Corporate Catering: Options and Prices

Food is one of the most important aspects of any corporate event. At WeBoat, we offer professional catering services prepared on board or delivered ready to serve:

BBQ on Board

The favorite format for Brazilian-style celebrations. All boats have a built-in grill, and the food is prepared by a professional grill master.

  • Per person menu: R$ 100 to R$ 160/person (~$20-32 USD, includes premium cuts, side dishes, dessert, and preparation)
  • Grill fee: R$ 250 to R$ 600 (~$50-120 USD, varies by boat)

Open Bar

Professional drinks served by a bartender on board. Explore all open bar options and prices.

Cheese and Wine Board

  • R$ 115 to R$ 140/person (~$23-28 USD)

BBQ + Open Bar Combo

The most complete package for all-inclusive celebrations:

Audio and Visual Equipment

For presentations, speeches, or simply setting the mood with music:

  • Bluetooth sound system: included on all boats -- connect your phone for a corporate playlist or audio presentations
  • TV / Portable projector: available on larger boats (such as the Rio Star 50) -- ideal for slide presentations, institutional videos, or year-in-review compilations
  • Wireless microphone: available upon request for speeches and group activities

For executive meetings, the anchored stretch at Mureta da Urca offers calm waters, ideal for moments that require focus and concentration.

Invoicing and Corporate Billing

We know corporate events require proper documentation. At WeBoat, the entire process is simple and transparent:

  • Invoice (Nota Fiscal): issued for all contracted services (boat, catering, decoration)
  • Company billing (CNPJ): the invoice can be issued under the contracting company's name
  • Booking deposit: 30% of the total amount at the time of booking
  • Remaining balance: can be paid up to the day of the event

Payment Methods

  • PIX -- instant bank transfer (preferred)
  • Credit card -- up to 3 interest-free installments
  • Bank transfer -- ideal for companies with procurement processes
  • Wire transfer

For procurement departments

We send a detailed commercial proposal by email with all itemized costs, making internal approval easier. We also work with purchase orders (PO) and service contracts if needed.

Team Building Activities on the Water

One of the great advantages of a corporate event on a boat is the opportunity to include water activities that promote integration and collaboration:

  • Diving and swimming competition: team challenges in the crystal-clear waters around Ilhas Cagarras or Praia Vermelha
  • Nautical Olympics: fun events such as float races, water ball toss, and tug-of-war in the water
  • Aquatic treasure hunt: a dynamic activity with clues leading to different points along the route
  • Group stand-up paddle: a team activity that builds balance and cooperation (available upon request)
  • Snorkeling: guided exploration of marine life (floating mats and pool noodles are included on all boats)
  • Reflection moment: group discussions anchored at scenic spots like Mureta da Urca, with a privileged view

All activities can be coordinated with the captain to ensure safety. Life jackets and emergency equipment are always available on board.

Planning Timeline: From First Contact to Event Day

To make sure everything goes perfectly, we recommend following this timeline:

2 months before

  • Define the date, number of attendees, and available budget
  • Contact WeBoat via WhatsApp to check availability
  • Receive and approve the commercial proposal
  • Pay the 30% deposit to secure the booking

1 month before

  • Confirm the catering menu (BBQ, open bar, cheese board)
  • Define AV equipment needs (TV, microphone)
  • Plan team building activities, if applicable
  • Communicate the event details to employees (dress code: beachwear/casual)
  • Request an advance invoice if needed

1 week before

  • Confirm the final number of attendees
  • Inform WeBoat of any dietary restrictions or special needs
  • Share how it works boarding information with attendees
  • Prepare your corporate playlist or presentation materials
  • Pay the remaining balance

On the day of the event

  • Arrive at Marina da Glória 15 minutes before the scheduled boarding time
  • The captain and catering team will already be on board with everything set up
  • Enjoy 5 hours of an exclusive event with your team
  • Capture the best moments -- Rio's scenery is perfect for corporate photos

Logistics and Schedule

WeBoat offers full flexibility to adapt the event to your company's schedule:

  • Flexible departure times: boarding anytime between 8am and 5pm (last departure to enjoy 5 hours with return before dark)
  • Standard duration: 5 hours -- enough for a complete corporate event with navigation, activities, a meal, and socializing
  • Extra hours: possible to extend at a proportional rate (inquire via WhatsApp)
  • Boarding point: Marina da Glória -- easy access by Uber/taxi, with parking on site
  • Weekdays: preferred for corporate events, with more affordable pricing (savings of R$ 400 to R$ 500, ~$80-100 USD compared to weekends)

Frequently Asked Questions About Corporate Events

How many people can attend a corporate event on a boat?

WeBoat's own fleet accommodates 12 to 22 people. For larger groups, we have partner boats with capacity for up to 65 people. It's also possible to split the group across two or more boats for a simultaneous event.

Does WeBoat issue invoices for corporate events?

Yes. WeBoat issues invoices (nota fiscal) for all contracted services. Billing can be done under the company name (CNPJ) and payment is accepted via PIX, credit card (up to 3 interest-free installments), and bank transfer.

How far in advance should I book a corporate event on a boat?

We recommend booking at least 30 to 60 days in advance, especially for events with catering and extra services. For peak season dates (December, January, holidays), booking 2 to 3 months ahead is ideal.

Is it possible to hold presentations or meetings on board?

Yes. All boats have Bluetooth sound systems for audio presentations. On larger boats like the WeBoat Rio Star 50, a portable TV or projector can be set up. For formal meetings, we recommend the anchored stretch at Mureta da Urca, which offers calm waters and an inspiring backdrop.
