Marina da Gloria Guide: Everything You Need to Know

If you are planning a boat trip in Rio de Janeiro with WeBoat Brasil, you will need to know Marina da Gloria. This is the departure point for all our trips, and understanding how the place works will make your experience much smoother and more enjoyable. In this guide, we have gathered everything you need to know: how to get there, where to park, what to expect on the day, and practical tips to make the most of it.

Location: Where is Marina da Gloria

Marina da Gloria is located in the Gloria neighborhood, in the South Zone of Rio de Janeiro, on the shores of Guanabara Bay. The exact address is Av. Infante Dom Henrique, S/N - Gloria, Rio de Janeiro - RJ, CEP 20021-140. The marina is inside the Aterro do Flamengo complex, one of the city's landmarks, with a privileged view of Sugarloaf Mountain and Morro da Urca.

The location is strategic: from there, boats have quick access to the main tourist spots along the Rio coastline, such as Mureta da Urca, Praia Vermelha, Copacabana, and Ilhas Cagarras.

How to Get to Marina da Gloria

There are several ways to get to Marina da Gloria, and all are relatively simple. Here are the options:

By car

If you are coming from the South Zone, follow Aterro do Flamengo towards Downtown. The Marina entrance is on the right, well signposted. If coming from Downtown or the North Zone, access via the same Aterro towards the South Zone. Enter "Marina da Gloria" in your GPS or navigation app and follow the directions.

By metro

The nearest station is Gloria Station, on Line 1 (orange). From there, it is approximately a 10-minute walk to the marina. The route is flat and pleasant, passing through one of the most beautiful areas of Aterro do Flamengo.

By Uber or 99

The most practical way for most people. Simply enter "Marina da Gloria" as your destination in the app. Drivers know the location well, and drop-off is at the marina entrance. Tip: agree on a meeting point with your group to make everyone's arrival easier.

Parking at Marina da Gloria

Marina da Gloria has its own parking lot, which is a great advantage for those driving. The average price is around R$ 30 to R$ 50 for the full day, depending on the time and period. On weekends and holidays, it can be busier, so we recommend arriving early to secure a spot.

For those who prefer not to drive, ride-hailing apps are the best alternative, especially if the group plans to have drinks during the trip.

WeBoat Brasil is located at Shop 06 at Marina da Gloria. When entering the marina, follow the shops in the commercial complex. Our team will be wearing WeBoat t-shirts, ready to welcome you. What to Expect on Trip Day

For everything to go perfectly, here is the step-by-step of what happens when you arrive for your trip:

  1. Arrive 15 minutes before the scheduled time. This ensures time for check-in without rushing.
  2. Check-in with the WeBoat team: our team verifies the reservation, answers last-minute questions, and organizes the group's belongings.
  3. Life jackets and safety instructions: everyone receives a life jacket and basic on-board safety guidelines. Safety is a priority on all our trips.
  4. Boarding at the pier: the team accompanies the group to the boat, assists with boarding, and introduces the skipper who will lead the trip.

From there, just enjoy. The skipper knows all the best spots along the Rio coastline and will ensure the trip is unforgettable.

Marina da Gloria Infrastructure

Marina da Gloria is much more than a boarding point. The complex offers full infrastructure for before and after your trip:

  • Restaurants and bars: there are several dining options around the marina, ideal for lunch before boarding or dinner after the trip.
  • Restrooms: the marina has restrooms available for visitors.
  • Flamengo Park: the largest urban waterfront park in the world is literally next door. It is worth arriving early for a walk or to enjoy the space with children.
  • Panoramic view: even before boarding, the view of Guanabara Bay, Sugarloaf Mountain, and Christ the Redeemer is already worth the trip.

Practical Tips for Trip Day

To make the most of your boat experience, here are some essential tips:

  • Sunscreen: essential. The sun at sea is much more intense than in the city. Bring high-factor sunscreen (SPF 50+) and reapply throughout the trip.
  • Light clothing: wear comfortable, lightweight clothes. Bikini, swim trunks, or swimwear underneath, with a cover-up or t-shirt on top.
  • Drinks and food: you can bring your own drinks and snacks for the boat's cooler (ice not included). For convenience, check our open bar, barbecue, and decoration service.
  • Towel and change of clothes: bring a towel and a dry change of clothes for the return.
  • Proper footwear: flip-flops or neoprene shoes are ideal. Avoid heeled shoes or dark-soled shoes that may mark the deck.
  • Electronics: bring your phone in a waterproof case. Cameras and drones are welcome, but always with extra care near the water.
WeBoat team tip: if it is the group's first time on a boat trip, relax. Our team takes care of everything so you only need to worry about having fun.
