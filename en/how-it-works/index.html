How Boat Rental Works

Simple and transparent process in 5 steps. From the first contact to the day of your trip, our team takes care of everything so you only have to worry about enjoying it.

How to rent a boat in Rio de Janeiro?

Renting a boat at WeBoat works in 5 simple steps: 1) Contact via WhatsApp, 2) Choose your boat and route, 3) Book with 50% deposit, 4) Arrive at Marina da Gloria, 5) Board and enjoy. The entire process takes less than 30 minutes and you can book in advance or even on the same day (subject to availability).

1

Contact us via WhatsApp

Send a message to (21) 97772-4114 with the desired date, number of people and occasion (birthday, bachelorette, family trip, etc.). Our team responds in minutes and sends boat and route suggestions for your group.

  • Service every day from 8am to 8pm
  • Response within 5 minutes
  • No commitment - ask your questions first
Start a Conversation
2

Choose the ideal boat and route

Based on your group size and preferences, we recommend the best boat and route combination. We have 5 own boats (10-22 people) and 21 partner boats (up to 65 people). You receive photos, videos and all the information to decide with confidence.

Own Boats

5 boats for 10 to 22 people. Priority scheduling and direct service from the WeBoat team.

View boats

Routes

6 routes of 5 hours. From Urca to Ilhas Cagarras, passing through Copacabana and Niteroi.

View routes
3

Book with a 50% deposit

Confirm your reservation by paying 50% via PIX, credit card or bank transfer. The remainder is paid on the day of the trip. You receive confirmation via WhatsApp with all details: date, time, boat and meeting point.

  • PIX: Instant payment
  • Credit Card: Up to 12 installments (card fees apply)
  • Bank Transfer: Wire transfer

Cancellation policy: Cancellations up to 48 hours before receive a full refund. In case of bad weather, we reschedule at no cost.

4

Arrive at Marina da Gloria

On the day of the trip, arrive 15 minutes before the scheduled time at Marina da Gloria (Av. Infante Dom Henrique, S/N, Shop 06 - Gloria, Rio de Janeiro). Paid parking available on site. Easy access via Aterro do Flamengo.

  • Marina da Gloria - Loja 06
  • Paid parking on site
  • Arrive 15 minutes early
5

Enjoy your trip!

Board the boat with your group, connect your playlist to the Bluetooth speakers and enjoy the sea of Rio de Janeiro. The sailor handles the navigation while you relax. All boats have floating mats, pool noodles, coolers and certified safety equipment.

What is included in every trip:

  • Fuel for the route
  • Licensed sailor
  • Bluetooth speakers
  • Coolers
  • Floating mat
  • Life jackets
