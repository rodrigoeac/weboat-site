What to expect from this trip?

The boat trip to Niteroi is our most exclusive route! Cross Guanabara Bay and discover the secluded beaches of Camboinhas and Itaipu, far from the crowds with total tranquility.

Unlike the other routes that stay in Rio's south zone, this trip takes you to the other side of the bay, revealing unexplored landscapes and beaches with calm, crystal-clear waters.

It is the perfect route for those seeking exclusivity, peace and wanting to escape the usual spots. The beaches of Camboinhas and Itaipu are true havens of tranquility just a few kilometers from downtown Rio.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions about the Itaipu and Camboinhas Trip

It is our most exclusive route! Unlike the others that stay in Rio's south zone, this trip crosses Guanabara Bay and takes you to secluded beaches in Niteroi.

They are semi-secluded beaches, much quieter than the south zone beaches. Since land access is more difficult, you find far fewer people, especially on weekdays.

The trip lasts 5 hours, which corresponds to a full shift. The Guanabara Bay crossing and sailing to the Niteroi beaches takes more time.

The price varies depending on the boat chosen and the day of the week. Contact us via WhatsApp for a personalized quote. All boats include fuel, captain, Bluetooth speakers and coolers.
