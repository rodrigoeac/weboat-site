What to expect from this trip?

The Full Tour is our most complete boat trip route in Rio de Janeiro! It is 5 hours of sailing combining the best spots of Guanabara Bay with the beaches of Niteroi in one unforgettable trip.

Departing from Marina da Gloria, the trip goes through Guanabara Bay passing by the Museum of Tomorrow, sailing under the Rio-Niteroi Bridge and around the MAC in Niteroi. Then, you make swimming stops at Morcego, Adão e Eva beaches, and return passing by the historic Forts of Santa Cruz, Laje and São João to Mureta da Urca, with a privileged view of Sugarloaf Mountain.

Perfect for special birthdays, important celebrations or simply for those who want to spend an entire day of pure fun and beauty on the Rio sea. We recommend combining it with BBQ and open bar for the complete experience!

Frequently Asked Questions about the Full Tour

The route departs from Marina da Gloria, passes by the Museum of Tomorrow, sails under the Rio-Niteroi Bridge, goes around the MAC in Niteroi, passes Icarai, makes swimming stops at Morcego, Adão e Eva and Mureta da Urca, and returns passing by the historic Forts.

The Full Tour lasts 5 hours, being our longest trip. It is ideal for those who want to spend a whole day at sea exploring the main landmarks of Rio and Niteroi.

There are 3 swimming stops: Morcego Beach, Adão e Eva Beaches, and Mureta da Urca. All in locations with calm, crystal-clear waters.

The price varies depending on the boat chosen and the day of the week. Contact us via WhatsApp for a personalized quote. We recommend combining it with BBQ and open bar for the complete experience!
