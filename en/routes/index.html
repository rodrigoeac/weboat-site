Frequently Asked Questions about Boat Trips

For a first time, we recommend the Mureta da Urca Route (5h) or Praia Vermelha (5h). Both are in calm waters within Guanabara Bay. Urca offers views of Sugarloaf Mountain and is ideal for families. See our available boats to choose the best option for your group.

Boat trips in Rio start from R$ 2,300 for groups of up to 15 people (5 hours). The price is per boat, not per person. Longer routes like Ilhas Cagarras (5h) start from R$ 3,600. Check our boats page for detailed pricing per vessel.

All trips include fuel, a Navy-licensed captain, floating mat, pool noodles, Bluetooth speakers and coolers. You can bring your own drinks and snacks at no extra charge. Extras like BBQ on the boat, decoration and open bar have an additional fee.

Yes, all routes can be customized. We work with suggestions but adapt according to your preferences. Want to combine Urca + Copacabana? Or extend the time? Talk to our team via WhatsApp to create the ideal trip.

Rio de Janeiro has favorable weather year-round. Summer (December to March) is peak season with calmer seas. Winter has lower prices. For open sea routes like Ilhas Cagarras, we prefer days with little wind. The team confirms conditions before the trip.

If there are adverse weather conditions, we reschedule at no extra charge or refund 100% of the amount. Our team monitors the forecast and notifies you in advance. Learn more on our frequently asked questions page.
